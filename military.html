Military & DoD

Protecting force readiness, one step at a time

Foot biomechanics ripple upward — into the knees, hips, spine, and gait efficiency that determine warfighter performance. CalSense brings real-time pressure and motion monitoring to injury prevention, readiness, and veteran care.

Injury Prevention

Prevention of musculoskeletal injuries (MSK)

MSK injuries carry a real operational cost — limited duty status, lost training days, medical discharge, and long-term disability payments. Foot biomechanics directly affect:

  • Knees
  • Hips
  • Spine
  • Gait Efficiency
  • Load Distribution

Readiness & Performance

Foot pain doesn't stay in the foot

Foot pain and lower-extremity injuries reduce march endurance, running speed, agility, balance, and deployment capability. Real-time monitoring could change that.

What foot pain costs

  • ⛔ Reduced march endurance
  • ⛔ Slower running speed
  • ⛔ Diminished agility & balance
  • ⛔ Lower deployment capability

What real-time monitoring enables

  • ✅ Detect fatigue before it becomes injury
  • ✅ Optimize soldier performance
  • ✅ Reduce overtraining
  • ✅ Personalize footwear & load carriage
