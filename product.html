Product & Technology

The Pressure Mapping Insole

An insole embedded with an abundance of pressure sensors, purpose-built to provide continuous pressure distribution data — transmitted wirelessly to a mobile app and turned into feedback that helps users relieve pressure before it becomes damage.

Overview

Engineered to disappear into everyday footwear

Our design incorporates a total of 160 pressure sensors as well as a 6-axis IMU to continuously monitor plantar pressure distribution. It's packaged to fit comfortably into conventional sneakers, powered by a rechargeable 3.7V Li-Ion battery for 10–12 hours of continuous use.

The BLE-operated controller module continuously transmits data wirelessly to a mobile application, where it can be reviewed and exported — giving users and clinicians the tools to assess risk and prevent long-term complications through continuous monitoring and early intervention.

Operation

Real-time continuous monitoring and analytics

From first step to actionable feedback — the full data path behind CalSense.

1

Capture

160 embedded pressure sensors and a 6-axis IMU continuously record plantar pressure distribution and motion as the user walks, stands, and moves through their day.

2

Transmit

The BLE controller module streams sensor data wirelessly and continuously to the CalSense mobile app — no docking or manual sync required.

3

Analyze

The platform builds a continuous picture of pressure distribution over time, flagging elevated-pressure zones and trends that precede tissue damage.

4

Act

Users and providers receive clear, actionable feedback — where to relieve pressure, when to check in, and what's changed since the last session.

Ease of Use

As simple as putting on your shoes

1. Insert

Treat the package as a conventional sole — slide it into each shoe.

2. Connect

Download the mobile app and follow the on-screen instructions to pair.

3. Wear

Go about your day — continuous monitoring runs quietly in the background.

4. Recharge

Top up through the USB-C port or a wireless charging pad.
